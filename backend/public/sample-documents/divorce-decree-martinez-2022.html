I. MARRIAGE DISSOLUTION

The marriage between Rebecca Lynn Martinez and Carlos Martinez, which commenced on August 20, 2010 in San Diego County, California, is hereby DISSOLVED. The Court finds that irreconcilable differences have led to the irremediable breakdown of the marriage. Date of separation: May 1, 2021.

II. CHILD CUSTODY AND VISITATION

Child: Sofia Martinez, born November 5, 2012 (Age 11)

Legal Custody: The parties shall share joint legal custody with joint decision-making authority on all major decisions affecting the child's education, health care, and religious upbringing.

Physical Custody: Primary physical custody is awarded to Petitioner (Rebecca Martinez). Respondent shall have visitation as follows: alternating weekends (Friday 6:00 PM through Sunday 6:00 PM), one weekday evening per week (Wednesday 5:00 PM to 8:00 PM), alternating major holidays, and three weeks during summer vacation.

III. CHILD SUPPORT

Respondent (Carlos Martinez) shall pay to Petitioner (Rebecca Martinez) child support in the amount of $1,200.00 per month, payable on the 1st day of each month, commencing April 1, 2022, and continuing until the child reaches age 18 or graduates from high school, whichever occurs later.

Respondent shall maintain health insurance coverage for the minor child. Unreimbursed medical expenses shall be split 50/50 between the parties. Childcare expenses shall be split 60% respondent, 40% petitioner. Private school tuition shall be split 60% respondent, 40% petitioner.

IV. SPOUSAL SUPPORT

Spousal support is WAIVED by mutual agreement of both parties. Each party has been found to be self-sufficient with adequate separate income.

V. DIVISION OF REAL PROPERTY

Primary Residence - 2847 Harbor View Lane, San Diego, CA 92101: Awarded to Petitioner. Petitioner shall refinance the existing mortgage within 180 days to remove Respondent from all liability. Respondent shall receive an equity buyout of $115,000.00, payable within 180 days of this decree.

VI. DIVISION OF PERSONAL PROPERTY

Vehicles: The 2019 BMW X3 is awarded to Petitioner, who shall assume the loan and remove Respondent from title. The 2020 Toyota Tacoma is awarded to Respondent, who shall assume the loan and remove Petitioner from title.

Business Assets: Martinez Design Studio LLC (EIN: 77-9876543) is awarded 100% to Petitioner (Rebecca Martinez). Respondent shall receive a one-time business buyout payment of $45,000.00, payable within 90 days of this decree.

VII. DIVISION OF FINANCIAL ACCOUNTS

Bank Accounts: Wells Fargo Business Checking (****3456) awarded 100% to Petitioner. Bank of America Joint Savings account ($68,000 balance) shall be divided equally ($34,000 each).

Retirement Accounts: Petitioner's SEP-IRA (~$145,000) awarded 100% to Petitioner as premarital and business asset. Respondent's 401(k) shall be divided via QDRO: marital portion split 50/50 (Petitioner receives $28,500).

VIII. DIVISION OF DEBTS

American Express Business Card ($8,500) - Petitioner's responsibility. Chase Sapphire Reserve ($3,200) - Petitioner's responsibility. Business Equipment Lease ($12,000) - Petitioner's responsibility. BMW Financial ($32,000) - Petitioner's responsibility. Discover Card ($5,400) - Respondent's responsibility. Toyota Financial ($28,500) - Respondent's responsibility.

IX. OTHER PROVISIONS

Both parties shall maintain life insurance of at least $250,000.00 naming the minor child as beneficiary while child support obligations exist. Tax exemption for the minor child shall alternate: Respondent claims in even years, Petitioner in odd years. Neither party may relocate the child outside San Diego County without court approval or written consent of the other party.

X. NAME RESTORATION

Petitioner elects to RETAIN the married name "Martinez."
