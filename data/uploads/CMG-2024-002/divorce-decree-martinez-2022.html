SUPERIOR COURT OF CALIFORNIA
COUNTY OF SAN DIEGO
CENTRAL DIVISION

JUDGMENT OF DISSOLUTION OF MARRIAGE
Case Number: 37-2021-00045678-CU-FL-CTL
Petitioner: Rebecca Lynn Martinez
Respondent: David Michael Martinez
Date of Judgment: March 15, 2022
I. DISSOLUTION OF MARRIAGE
The Court finds that all statutory requirements have been met, including residency and irreconcilable differences. The marriage between Rebecca Lynn Martinez and David Michael Martinez is hereby dissolved, effective immediately.
II. CHILD CUSTODY AND VISITATION
The parties have one minor child: Emma Sofia Martinez, born June 15, 2014 (age 7).

Legal Custody: Joint legal custody is awarded to both parents.
Physical Custody: Primary physical custody is awarded to Petitioner (Rebecca Martinez). Respondent shall have visitation every other weekend and one evening per week.
III. CHILD SUPPORT
Respondent (David Martinez) is ordered to pay child support to Petitioner in the amount of $1,200.00 per month, due on the first day of each month, beginning April 1, 2022.
Child support shall continue until the child reaches age 18 and graduates from high school, or age 19 if still attending high school full-time, or until further court order.
Payments shall be made through the San Diego County Child Support Services Department.
IV. SPOUSAL SUPPORT
The Court finds that both parties are self-supporting and have the ability to maintain the standard of living established during the marriage through their own employment and income. No spousal support is awarded to either party. Each party's right to spousal support is terminated and cannot be reserved for future modification.
V. DIVISION OF PROPERTY
Real Property: The family residence located at 2847 Harbor View Lane, San Diego, CA 92101 is awarded to Petitioner (Rebecca Martinez), subject to existing mortgage. Respondent's name shall be removed from title within 60 days.
Personal Property and Assets: All personal property, bank accounts, retirement accounts, and vehicles have been divided by written agreement of the parties, which is incorporated herein by reference.
VI. DIVISION OF DEBTS
Each party shall be responsible for debts in their own name. The mortgage on the family residence shall be the sole responsibility of Petitioner.
VII. NAME CHANGE
Petitioner elects to retain the name "Martinez" for personal and professional continuity reasons.

IT IS SO ORDERED.

Hon. Jennifer L. Thompson
Judge of the Superior Court
